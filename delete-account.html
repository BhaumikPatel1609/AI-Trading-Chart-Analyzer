Delete Account

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Option 1: Delete from App

  1. Open the AI Trading Chart Analyzer app
  2. Go to Settings / Profile
  3. Tap on "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

Option 2: Request via Email (Required by Google)

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by contacting us:

📧 Email: nextgenappcore@gmail.com

Please include your registered email address in the request.

What happens after deletion?

  • Your account will be permanently deleted
  • Your authentication data will be removed
  • Your local data will be deleted from your device

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
